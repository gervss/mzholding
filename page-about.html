Company overview

An international investment group whose mission is the successful management of resources to create and develop creative business opportunities and solutions. Our investments generally produce a balance between business, social and economical accomplishment, to equally serve the interest of investors and consumers. While based primarily in the Middle East and Northern Africa (MENA) region our company has offices on three (3) Continents and over 700 dedicated employees. Our firm is poised to tackle any project and execute fully the requirements of any of our Customers, partners, or business associates. The group consists of nine companies with a very diverse approach to the world’s economic society.
